Our Story

From Nearly Closing
to Phoenix's
Favorite Café.

Cafe Mollie's story is one of courage. When the café was on the verge of closing, the owners made a bold bet: go all in on Vietnamese coffee. They went back to their roots, refined every recipe, and built something the Valley had never quite seen before.

Today, Cafe Mollie is a beloved fixture on North 16th Street — featured in AZCentral and beloved by hundreds of loyal regulars. Every drink is made by hand. Every pastry is baked before sunrise. And the welcome is always warm.

This is what happens when passion and craft meet community.
